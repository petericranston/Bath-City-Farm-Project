Community Projects Actvities
At Bath City Farm

Get involved with our volunteer teams, or join one of our therapeutic well-being groups. We also offer specialist groups for adults with additional needs. From toddlers under 5 to children, and young people up to 16 we have the group to suit. Get closer to nature with our fun activities and games. Leave your desk behind, come down to the farm and take part in activities in the fresh air and build skills, confidence and morale.

Choose to host your birthday party in our indoor timber framed room. You can invite up to 15 children for children aged 6 and over, or for children under 6 years, you can invite up to 10 children with a maximum of 1 carer per child. Hire includes, tables and chairs. The indoor timber framed room is a minutes walk from the car park. Toilets are in the main building, a very short walk away.

We have several spaces for hire at the farm. Celebrate a birthday in our outdoor shelter, book our meeting room or hold a performance in our 60 seater amphitheatre.
